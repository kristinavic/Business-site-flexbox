About us

Matthew

I am a passionate artisan who began exploring leathercrafting as a creative pursuit in my spare time. What began as a personal interest has since evolved into a small business dedicated to creating high-quality, handcrafted leather goods. Each piece is meticulously crafted with attention to detail, combining traditional techniques with contemporary design. I take great pride in offering products that reflect my dedication to craftsmanship and quality, and I am committed to growing my craft while providing exceptional items for my customers.

Katherine

She is a creative and driven professional who oversees the marketing and design aspects of our business. With a keen eye for design and a passion for strategic communication, she is responsible for developing our brand identity and managing the website. Her expertise helps ensure that our online presence accurately reflects our values and the quality of our handmade products.
